How do we operate?

We Evaluate your current marketing efforts to determine where we're at, Define our goals and metrics to ensure success, and build a Strategy to achieve your email objectives.

We kick start each project with a Discovery & Strategy phase to determine your main objectives.

We'll look at:

  • Current marketing efforts

  • Store sessions and sources

  • Email list health

  • Important metrics like CRV, AOV, and more.

This will help us understand what’s been done, what works, and what we need to do next.

Based on the evaluation we define KPIs that align with your marketing goals. We answer questions like:

  • What will make this a successful campaign?

  • Who is your ideal audience?

  • What does the market in your industry look like?

  • What KPIs and deadlines do we need to meet on our roadmap?

With the information gathered we build a Strategy that takes you from where you are now to where you want to be.

  • Increase customer lifetime value

  • Boost average order value

  • Receive more product reviews

  • Build meaningful customer relationships

  • Optimize conversions with email automation
